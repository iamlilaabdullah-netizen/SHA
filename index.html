Preamble

We, the students of Michigan State University, recognizing the critical importance of affordable, safe, and accessible housing for the well-being and success of the student body, do hereby establish this Constitution for the Spartan Housing Alliance in pursuit of advocacy, education, and community empowerment in housing justice.

Mission & Purpose

  • Advocate for policies and initiatives that promote affordable, safe, and accessible housing options for all MSU students.
  • Educate students and the broader community on critical housing issues.
  • Empower students to engage in housing-related advocacy efforts.
  • Collaborate with administration, government, and community partners.
